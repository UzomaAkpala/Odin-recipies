Gucamole

Guacamole is basically mashed, raw avocados mixed with other ingredients to taste.
avocados give guacamole the green base, and other ingredients, such as tomatoes and onions give it some textured flavor.
Guacamole is especially popular on Super Bowl Sunday and Cinco de Mayo.

Ingredients

    These are the ingredients you'll need to make this irresistible guacamole recipe:

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl;
    mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.

  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
